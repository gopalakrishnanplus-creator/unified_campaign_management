The ticketing system
The ticketing system automatically assigns a ticket to a department in Inditech and
also sends the ticket by email to the department email address specified in the
system.
A ticket can be closed only by the direct recipient of the ticket from the ticketing
system. The ticket can be delegated to another email address by the recipient or
reassigned to the sender by the recipient. Every recipient of a ticket, whether a
direct recipient or a delegated recipient, can add notes to the ticket. Each note is
automatically time-tagged, and they can also add supporting material to their note
in the form of file uploads, limited to 5 MB per file, maximum of three file
attachments per note. The ticket has status flags which only the direct recipient
can change. These status flags could be completed, in process, cannot be
completed, on hold, not started. 
The ticketing system database retains the status and details of all tickets that have
been raised so far. 
Every recipient or a delegated recipient has an account on the ticketing system
and can log in using their email address along with Google Auth. They see a
dashboard which gives them all the tickets that have been raised on them, which
can be filtered by status and by period, etc. 
The project manager has the ability to Add users, Remove users and perform
other admin activities that a ticketing system usually requires.
