Video Selection & Search Share Patient Education Do I need to use the ﬁlters before searching? Using ﬁlters is optional; you may use them if

Video Selection & Search Share Patient Education What is the difference between Therapy Area,
Trigger, and Bundle?
Therapy Area refers to the medical category,
Trigger refers to a speciﬁc condition or
situation, and Bundle is a group of related
videos under a common category or trigger.
Video Selection & Search Share Patient Education Is the searched video already selected?
No, searching only ﬁlters the results. The
doctor can choose to select an individual video
or the entire bundle it belongs to.
Video Selection & Search Share Patient Education What is the difference between Select Bundle
and Select Video?
“Select Bundle” allows sharing multiple videos
within the same group, trigger, or therapy area,
while “Select Video” allows sharing a single
video.
Video Selection & Search Share Patient Education Can I send only one video instead of the whole
bundle?
Yes, you can send a single video by using the
“Select Video” option.

the preferred language for the message and
the video/video bundle shared with the patient.

Message What message will the patient receive?
The patient will receive a WhatsApp message
that includes clinic branding, the doctor’s
message, the video title, and a link to the
patient video page.
WhatsApp Message Sharing Patient WhatsApp
Message Does the patient get a direct link to the video?
Yes, the WhatsApp message includes a direct
link to a page where the video is available
below the title.

Yes, the WhatsApp message clearly displays
the video title before the link, helping the
patient identify the video.
