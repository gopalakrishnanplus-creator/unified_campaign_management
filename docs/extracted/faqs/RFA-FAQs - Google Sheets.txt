Are Doctor PDF and Patient PDF passwords different?
Yes. As a doctor, your PDF password is based on your own ﬁrst name and phonenumber, while the patient’s PDF password is based on the patient’s ﬁrst name andphone number.
Example:
Doctor: First 4 letters of your ﬁrst name + last 4 digits of your phone →  e.g., kuma2357
Patient: First 4 letters of the patient’s ﬁrst name + last 4 digits of their phone →  e.g.,kaus7277
This way, your PDF and the patient’s PDF each have a unique password.
